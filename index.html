Why Test and Tag?

Testing and tagging ensures that every electrical appliance in your workplace is working as intended and does not pose any risk to your customers or employees. Electrical faults can cause injuries and downtime, and can often be prevented with regular inspections.

Our testing procedures are fully compliant with the AS/NZS 3760:2010 standards, which set out the appropriate methods and timelines for inspecting, testing and tagging electrical appliances. Our testing equipment allows us to quickly test appliances according the to specific requirements of each appliance, document the result for your records and apply a tag to show the item is compliant.
