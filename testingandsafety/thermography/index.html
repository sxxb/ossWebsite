Thermography

Thermography (or thermal imaging) is used to measure the radiant heat patterns which are emitted from electrical components. This can help identify faulty or at-risk components before they cause significant damage.

Our Thermal Imaging Services

We can conduct thermal imaging on appliances and machinery to identify points of excessive heat, which may indicate a potential fault. Thermography is often used as a proactive diagnostic tool, which allows the detection of developing faults, before they cause system failure or emergency situation. By identifying problems before they become critical you can save time and money.

Benefits of Thermography

  • Identify specific component issues within systems
  • Replace only faulty components, rather than whole sections of a system
  • Reduce unexpected maintenance costs
  • Schedule proactive maintenance, rather than pay additional costs for emergency or unscheduled repairs
  • Reduce risk of blackouts, system failures, downtime, and emergencies
  • Increase safety of workers and customers
