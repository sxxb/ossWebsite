Testing & Safety

OSS offer a range of electrical testing and safety services to help keep your workplace safe for you, your workers and your customers. With regular safety testing you can prevent accidents from occuring and avoid injuries or downtime from affecting your operations.

Testing & Tagging

Testing and tagging ensures that every electrical appliance in your workplace is working as intended and does not pose any risk to your customers or employees.

RCD Testing

Residual Current Devices, or RCDs, are devices that quickly break electrical circuits when the supply current doesn't match the return current. Regular testing of fixed and portable RCDs ensures they operate correctly and prevent leakage current from causing harm.

Microwave Leakage Testing

Regular testing can ensure that microwave radiation is not leaking from your microwave appliances.

Thermography

Thermography (or thermal imaging) is used to measure the radiant heat patterns which are emitted from electrical components. This can help identify faulty or at-risk components before they cause significant damage.

Light Level Testing

Providing adequate lighting for your workplace is a key OH&S requirement, and can prevent accidents from occuring.

Emergency & Exit Light Testing
