The Benefits of Switching to LED Lighting

  • Energy efficiency – LEDs use a fraction of the energy of other lighting solutions, reducing your energy bills and reducing carbon emissions
  • Long life – LED lights can last over 50,000 hours, while similar brightness incandescent light bulbs typically last only 1,000 hours
  • Durability – LED lights have no fragile filament to contend with, and no fragile tube. They are resistant to heat, cold, and shock
  • Responsiveness - LEDs light up instantly to their maximum brightness, and do not need a warm up period like other types of lighting
  • Reliability - LEDs are ideal for use in applications that are subject to frequent on-off cycling, unlike fluorescent lamps that burn out more quickly when cycled frequently
