Electrical Contracting

We provide a range of electrical services to business from supply connections, communications, electrical, ICT, upgrades, Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS), cable support systems, to energy management systems. Whatever you require, either domestic, business, government or public venues we are your first choice for office upgrades, new fitouts, data centres, and computer rooms etc. We provide ‘round-the-clock’ 24/7 support for installations, upgrades and preventative work.

Our Electrical Contracting Services

We can provide a range of electrical services including:

  • Installation of Lighting, power points and switchboards
  • External & Security Lighting (LED Lighting)
  • Installations of Smoke detectors
  • Shop floor lighting (LED Lighting)
  • and more!

Our Electrical Maintenance Services

OSS can also provide a range of electrical maintenance services. Electrical maintenance is one of the easiest ways to avoid electrical faults. By conducting regular upkeep of electrical devices you can extend their lifetime and identify faults before they become critical.

  • Lighting Repair & Replacement
  • Repair and upgrade of electrical switchboard
  • Installation, service of smoke detectors
  • Maintenance of Power circuits
  • Security lighting, installations, repair & maintenance
  • Three phase power as well as single phase
  • Fault finding service
  • Supply & installation of RCDs (safety switches)
  • Ceiling & Exhaust fans installation & repairs
  • UPS – installs and supply (backup power for essential services e.g servers)
  • Thermography – including site visits, reports, preventative maintenance programs and Insurance requests
