Privacy Policy

As used herein, the term “Occupational Safety Services” is the trading name registered legally under the state of South Australia. The term “occsafetyservices.com” refers to the website owned and operated by Occupational Safety Services, whose home page resides on the Internet globally at www.occsafetyservices.com.

Your privacy on the Internet is of importance to us. At occsafetyservices.com, we want to make your experience online satisfying and safe. Because we gather certain types of information about our users, we feel you should fully understand the terms and conditions surrounding the capture and use of that information. This privacy statement discloses what information we gather and how we use it.

Please also be informed that these terms and conditions may be updated at any time. You may check back occasionally for updates. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.

1. Personal Information

Occupational Safety Services is committed to complying with Australia’s Privacy Act. Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to enable us to perform tasks such as responding to your requests, or to contact you via mail, e-mail or phone to assist you with any enquiries regarding products, services and applicable warranties we offer. We also use your personal information for marketing purposes such as to notify you of promotions, services, competitions and events we may offer/hold unless you have advised us in writing that you do not wish to receive this information. Occupational Safety Services never shares newsletter mailing lists with any third parties, including advertisers, sponsors or partners.

You can request to access your personal information at anytime to update or correct the information we hold on you by email, post or phone. Your personal information will not be disclosed to a third party without your permission.

Occupational Safety Services work to protect the security of your information where possible using industry standard firewall and password protection systems. Occupational Safety Services will continue to take measures to prevent unauthorized access to, improper use and the inaccuracy of your personal information. Please contact us immediately should you suspect that your account with us has been compromised as we take this very seriously.

5. Cookies

Like many web sites, we use ‘cookies’ and obtain certain types of information when your Web browser accesses our website. This enables our system to recognize your browser and to provide features such as storage of items in your Shopping Cart between visits. We use tracking information to determine which areas of our sites users like and don’t like based on traffic to those areas. We do not track what individual users read, but rather how well each page performs overall. This helps us continue to build a better service for you. We track search terms entered in the search function as one of many measures of what interests our users. We do not track the terms a particular user enters.

Occupational Safety Services creates aggregate reports on user demographics and traffic patterns for advertisers, sponsors and partners. This allows our advertisers to advertise more effectively, and allows our users to receive advertisements that are pertinent to their needs. Because we don’t track the usage patterns of individual users, an advertiser or sponsor will never know that a specific user clicked on their ad. We will not disclose any information about any individual user except to comply with applicable law or valid legal process or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public.
